  • English Sheepdogs are basically clouds with paws, and they put their fluff to good use by herding sheep with a style that's both efficient and cozy.

    They have a knack for finding treats and toys buried in the fluff, turning every day into an exciting scavenger hunt.

    Every year is a bad hair year for English Sheepdogs, but they wear it with pride, reminding us that elegance can be found in even the wildest of locks.

    With their perpetual bedhead and goofy expressions, English Sheepdogs are the stand-up comedians of the dog world, providing endless laughs.

    These fashion-forward furballs are the pioneers of the “shaggy chic” look, making them the envy of every other canine on the block.
