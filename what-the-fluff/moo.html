  • Scottish Highland cows come with built-in bad hair days, sporting luscious, unruly locks that make even the wildest of human bedheads jealous.

    They've got that "zen" thing down pat. Highland cows can strike a meditative pose with those relaxed, doleful expressions that say, "I'm one with the field."

    Highland cows are the original weather forecasters. Rain or shine, they're always prepared, thanks to their natural raincoat-like fur.

    Forget teddy bears; Highland cows are the real snuggle buddies. With those soulful eyes and fluffy bodies, they're basically walking, mooing pillows.

    Ever heard a Scottish Highland cow choir? Neither have we, but we're pretty sure they'd give bagpipes a run for their money in the melodious department.
