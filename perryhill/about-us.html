About Us

Perryhill Farm offers U-Pick apples, blueberries, cherries, peaches, plums, and raspberries.

A Unique Location

Our farm's unique location in Perrydale exposes our fruits to the summer diurnal effect. On most hot days, the ocean air spills through the Van Duzer Corridor into Perrydale starting in the late afternoon and continuing until dark. This dramatic cooling causes a higher sugar content in ripening fruit. Our higher elevation and hilly ground allows killing frost to roll downhill during bloom. We often set a crop when lower valley areas don't. In addition our hill soils aren't as rich as the valley soil. This causes slight stress on the fruit that leads to increased sugar and taste levels.

OUR FRUIT

Apples
Our orchards feature Royal Gala, Jonagold, Ida Red, and Honeycrisp varieties.

Blueberries
We have seven varieties across multiple fields, starting with Early Blue and ending with Elliott.

Cherries
We have over 15 varieties, including favorites such as Bing, Rainer, and Van, as well as new selections to try like Sandra Rosa and Regina.

Peaches
With 30 different varieties ranging from old favorites like Elberta, Veteran, and Red Haven to the new Flaming Fury and Steller series, we have a peach for any taste.

Plums
Our plum orchards feature both Brooks and Thundercloud varieties.

Raspberries
Our fields hold the PNW favorites of Cascade Bounty and Willamette.
